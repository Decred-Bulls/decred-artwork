Information to consider when editing text in the TwitterBanner.ai file:

- Text is located in the 'Footer' layer
- Layer opacity is at 90%
- Distance between the left and right text blocks is approximately the same as between the decred logo and the "deCRED"
- Font is Source Sans Variable
- Font size is 28pt
- Font weight is 500 (except "$10M+ in funds", which is 400 - the same as font style 'Regular')
- Font tracking is 70
